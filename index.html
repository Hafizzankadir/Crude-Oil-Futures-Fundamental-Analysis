NOTE Methodology

This desk mirrors the layered approach institutional crude desks use: supply data (OPEC+ quotas, shale, spare capacity), demand data (refinery runs, China imports, PMI), inventory prints (EIA/API/Cushing), positioning (CFTC COT), geopolitical risk premium, macro/FX (DXY, real rates), and volatility/flow signals — synthesized into one composite bias rather than traded in isolation.

All figures on this page are illustrative placeholders for framework purposes. To wire in live data, the natural sources are the EIA Open Data API (api.eia.gov) for inventories and production, the CFTC Commitments of Traders API (publicreporting.cftc.gov) for positioning, and FRED (fred.stlouisfed.org) for DXY and rate series.
